Release notes for Gaia v1.9.1 November 2018

Enhancements
· Reduced far clip plane to a max distance of 2248m on large terrains to improve framerate.
· Added new global reflection probe to scene to improve overall scene lighting
· Added camera follow reflection probe to follow camera, with adjustable height offset to adjust for reflection issues with trees.
· Modified reflection probe update algorithm to stop tearing.
· Improved large terrain auto settings to stop tops of mountains from being cut off.
· Improved water settings and reflections to stop blown out sun highlights.
· Impoved speed of quadtree system with resulting tree placement speed improvements.
· Fixed issue where post processing v2 was already in scene.
· Fixed linux and mac backslash issue.
· Imropoved 2018.3 compatibility.
· Fixed post processing gizmo.
