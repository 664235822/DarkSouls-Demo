GeNa Spawners
=============

Here is a set of spawners that have been pre-configured for use with GeNa.

GeNa gives you intuitive control over where your content is placed into your scene.

To use them first install GeNa, and then unzip the spawners and then drage the prefabs into your scene.

You can leanr more about GeNa here : http://www.procedural-worlds.com/gena/
